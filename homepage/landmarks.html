  1. The Wawel Castle: Open Maps

    2. You cannot go to Krakow and NOT visit the Wawel Castle! It is just below the main town square.

    This castle belonged to some of the greatest kings in Polish history. The architecture and design is beautiful!

  2. St. Mary's Basilica: Open Maps

    3. St. Mary's Basilica was built in the 14th century. It is a prime example of early Polish gothic architecture.

  3. Adam Mickiewicz Monument: Open Maps

    4. This is one of the best known monuments in Poland. It is located in the center of the main town square.

    Adam Mickiewicz was a beloved poet and a founding father of Polish Romanticism.

  4. The Wawel Dragon Open Maps

    5. The Wawel Dragon is famous for the folk tales people have told about it.

    Every 15 minutes the dragon breathes out fire from its mouth!
